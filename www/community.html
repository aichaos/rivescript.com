Community

Here are some ways you can get involved with the RiveScript community.

Botmakers Slack

Several contributors and users of RiveScript hang out on the Botmakers Slack organization, and there is a dedicated #rivescript channel there.

Botmakers is a general bot making community with all sorts of people and has over a thousand members.

Join Botmakers on Slack

Chatbots.org Forum

Chatbots.org is a web forum about chatbots and has a dedicated forum for RiveScript, in addition to AIML, ChatScript, and general chatbot topics.

 
 

Go to the Chatbots.org RiveScript Forum
